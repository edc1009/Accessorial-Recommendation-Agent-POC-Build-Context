# User Persona: Sam the Shipping Specialist

## 1. Overview
Sam is a **Shipping Specialist** (or Logistics Coordinator) working in the operations department of a mid-sized manufacturing, wholesale, or e-commerce company. Sam's primary responsibility is to ensure that products reach customers efficiently, safely, and cost-effectively.

## 2. Demographic Information
- **Role**: Shipping Specialist / Logistics Coordinator / Operations Manager.
- **Tools**: FreightPOP (TMS), ERP system, carrier portals, email.
- **Experience**: 2-5 years in logistics or supply chain management.
- **Work Environment**: Fast-paced office or warehouse setting.

## 3. Core Responsibilities
- booking 50+ **LTL (Less Than Truckload)** shipments daily.
- Choosing the right carrier based on cost and reliability.
- Entering shipment details (weight, package type, destination) into the FreightPOP system.
- Communicating with warehouse teams and customers regarding delivery status.
- Reviewing and approving carrier invoices.

## 4. Goals and Motivations
- **Accuracy**: Ensure every shipment is booked with the correct services to prevent delivery delays.
- **Cost Control**: Avoid "surprise" charges (accessorials) that were not quoted upfront.
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Ensure smooth deliveries so customers aren't bothered by extra charges or rescheduling requests.
- **Efficiency**: Spend as little time as possible on manual data entry and "checking" every single address.

## 5. Frustrations and Pain Points
- **Unexpected Invoices**: Receiving a bill from a carrier 30 days later that includes a $150 "Residential Delivery" or "Liftgate" fee that wasn't included in the original quote.
- **Margin Erosion**: Having to explain to the finance team why a shipment's profit was wiped out by missed accessorial fees.
- **Rebilling Disputes**: The awkward process of trying to bill a customer for a service (like a liftgate) weeks after they received their goods.
- **Incomplete Information**: Often booking shipments with just an address, without knowing if the destination has a loading dock or is a limited-access site (like a school).

## 6. How the Accessorial Recommendation Agent Helps Sam
- **Real-time Guardrail**: Flags potential missed services *during* the shipment creation process, before the quote is finalized.
- **Transparent Reasoning**: Provides clear, data-driven explanations (e.g., "Shipment is 500 lbs to a residential address; liftgate required") which Sam can use to justify the cost to the customer immediately.
- **Confidence Boost**: Reduces the "fear of the unknown" when shipping to new locations by suggesting limited-access or residential designations based on location type.
- **Decision Support**: Instead of Sam having to memorize every carrier's specific weight threshold for liftgate services, the agent does the "heavy lifting" by applying consistent industry rules.

## 7. Success for Sam
Success means booking a shipment in FreightPOP and knowing that the **quoted price matches the final invoice 100% of the time**, with zero surprise charges or customer complaints.
